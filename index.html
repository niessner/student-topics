Master Theses, Guided Research, IDP Topics

Visual Computing & Artificial Intelligence Lab

The following topics give a rough guideline for students interested in pursuing academic research in our lab. By definition, all of our topics are focused towards a potential academic contribution that is publishable. Please also let us know if you are interested in topics that are not directly listed here — our topics are flexible and can be tailored towards your skill sets.

To apply, please reach out directly to the supervisor listed on the project you are interested in. Include a short motivation, your CV, and a current transcript of records so we can quickly assess fit. Feel free to contact multiple supervisors if several topics appeal to you.
